Mostly Apples Apple Cake


Just as there are chocolate chip cookies for chip lovers and pies for crust lovers, there are apple cakes for apple lovers. Specifically, this Mostly Apples Apple Cake. (Perhaps the name gave it away?)An apple cake meant for apple lovers. With a custardy texture that's more akin to clafoutis or Dutch baby than to pound cake, this dessert showcases the fruit's flavor from bright to tart to honeyed to lemony, depending on the varieties you choose offset by a light, almond-scented batter.
